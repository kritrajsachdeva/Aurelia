Our Story

The Beginning

A Fashion House Born From Restraint

AURELIA was founded in 2015 by creative director Sophia Laurent in the heart of Milan. What began as a small atelier on Via della Spiga has grown into a globally recognised name synonymous with quiet luxury and precision tailoring.

Our philosophy has remained unchanged since day one: create pieces that outlive trends, that feel as relevant a decade from now as they do today. We design for real lives — women and men who dress with intention, who value craft over flash.

Every collection begins in our Milan studio, where a small team of specialist makers sketch, drape and refine each garment by hand before production begins. Nothing leaves the atelier without passing through the hands of at least three master craftspeople.

What We Stand For

Our Pillars

Precision Craft

Every garment passes through at least three master craftspeople before it bears the AURELIA label. We hold our standards to the highest measure.

Considered Sourcing

We partner exclusively with certified ethical mills and use natural fibres — silk, linen, cashmere, and Tencel — that are kind to the earth and the wearer.

Timeless Design

We don't chase trends. Our collections are designed to transcend seasons — pieces you will reach for year after year, decade after decade.
